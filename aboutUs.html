About us

Founder/Chief Physiotherapist

Dr. Nasreen is a dedicated Physiotherapy professional specializing in physical rehabilitation and mental wellness support. With a compassionate approach, she focuses on delivering personalized care that promotes both physical recovery and emotional balance.

Vision / Mission

OUR VISION
To create a trusted wellness center where patients feel supported, safe, and empowered throughout their recovery journey.

OUR MISSION
To provide holistic and evidence-based care that supports healing of both body and mind.

OUR VALUES
We are guided by compassion, integrity, respect, and excellence, ensuring every individual receives personalized, ethical, and empowering care in a nurturing environment.

Treatment philosophy / approach

Our treatment approach begins with a detailed assessment to understand the root cause of the condition. Based on individual needs, a customized therapy plan is designed focusing on pain relief, mobility improvement, and emotional well-being. Continuous monitoring ensures steady progress and long-term recovery.

Clinic Story

Dr. Nasreen PT Clinic was established with the vision of providing integrated care that addresses both physical health and emotional wellness. Recognizing the connection between body and mind, the clinic aims to deliver compassionate care that improves overall quality of life.

Certifications

Dr. Nasreen holds a Bachelor’s degree in Physiotherapy and has professional experience in treating musculoskeletal conditions, injury rehabilitation, and stress-related concerns. She combines clinical expertise with a patient-centered approach to ensure effective and safe treatment outcomes.
